1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
This policy establishes the standard process for onboarding all new employees, including
permanent hires, fixed-term contractors, and internal transfers. It defines the
responsibilities of HR, line managers, IT, and the new employee to ensure a consistent
and compliant start-of-employment experience across all UK offices and remote locations.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

2.1 HR Business Partner (HRBP)
Owns the end-to-end onboarding process. Initiates the New Employee Record in the
HR Information System (Workday) upon receipt of a signed offer letter. Sends the
Welcome Pack to the new employee at least five business days before the start date.
Coordinates with Payroll, IT, and the Line Manager to complete all pre-boarding tasks.

2.2 Talent Acquisition Team
Responsible for completing background checks via the Background Screening System
(Sterling) before the start date. Provides the HRBP with a Clearance Certificate
once all checks are passed. If a check is outstanding on the start date, the
Talent Acquisition Team escalates to the HR Director for a risk assessment.

2.3 Line Manager
Must complete the Line Manager Onboarding Checklist in Workday at least three
business days before the new employee's start date. Responsibilities include
assigning a Buddy, booking the 30-60-90 Day Review meetings, and ensuring the
new employee's workstation is prepared.

2.4 IT Department
Provisions all necessary hardware and software access based on the role profile
submitted by the HR Business Partner via the IT Service Management System (ServiceNow).
Laptop, access cards, and system credentials must be ready by 09:00 on the employee's
first day. Provisioning is tracked via a ServiceNow ticket linked to the employee record.

2.5 Payroll Team
Sets up the new employee on the Payroll System (ADP) using data transferred
automatically from Workday via the HR-Payroll Integration. Confirms National Insurance,
tax code, and bank details with the employee during the first week. Issues first payslip
through the Employee Self-Service Portal.

2.6 Buddy
A peer-level employee assigned by the Line Manager to provide informal support during
the first 90 days. Not a management role; responsible for social integration,
answering day-to-day questions, and checking in weekly.

2.7 New Employee
Responsible for completing all mandatory onboarding tasks in Workday within the
first five business days, including submitting bank details, reviewing the Employee
Handbook, and completing the mandatory e-learning modules in the Learning Management
System (Cornerstone).

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
3. PRE-BOARDING PROCESS (Before Day 1)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Step P1 – Offer Acceptance and Record Creation
The Talent Acquisition Team marks the candidate as "Offer Accepted" in the Applicant
Tracking System (ATS – Greenhouse). This triggers an automated integration that
creates a draft New Employee Record in Workday. The HRBP is notified via a Workday
workflow task.

Step P2 – Background Screening
The Talent Acquisition Team initiates a background check request in Sterling, covering
right-to-work verification, criminal record check (DBS), and employment history
verification. Sterling returns results within five business days. The HRBP monitors
check status via the Sterling dashboard.

Step P3 – Welcome Pack
The HRBP sends the Welcome Pack email to the new employee, containing:
- Start date, location, and first-day instructions
- Link to the Employee Self-Service Portal
- IT equipment delivery confirmation
- Pre-reading: Company Values, Code of Conduct, and Health & Safety Policy

Step P4 – IT Provisioning Request
The HRBP submits an IT provisioning request in ServiceNow, attaching the Role Profile
which specifies required system access (e.g. Microsoft 365, Salesforce CRM, Jira).
The IT Department fulfils the request and closes the ServiceNow ticket upon completion.

Step P5 – Payroll Setup
Workday automatically transfers the new employee's personal data to ADP via the
HR-Payroll Integration on the day the employment record is activated. The Payroll
Team receives a notification and verifies that all mandatory fields (NI number,
tax code, cost centre) are populated.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
4. FIRST DAY PROCESS (Day 1)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Step D1 – Welcome and Orientation
The Line Manager greets the new employee and provides a site orientation. The HRBP
conducts a 30-minute HR Induction Session covering: employment terms, benefits
enrolment, absence reporting, grievance and disciplinary policy, and data protection
(GDPR) obligations.

Step D2 – System Access Verification
The IT Department provides the new employee with login credentials for Microsoft 365
and the Employee Self-Service Portal. The new employee verifies access to all systems
listed in the ServiceNow provisioning ticket. Any access issues are reported to the
IT Service Desk within the same day.

Step D3 – Mandatory e-Learning Enrolment
The Learning Management System (Cornerstone) automatically enrols the new employee
in mandatory modules based on their role and department. Required modules include:
- Information Security Awareness (due within 5 days)
- Health & Safety at Work (due within 5 days)
- Anti-Bribery and Corruption (due within 10 days)
- GDPR and Data Protection (due within 10 days)
Non-completion triggers an automated reminder from Cornerstone after the due date,
escalating to the Line Manager if still outstanding after 5 days.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
5. FIRST 90 DAYS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Step F1 – 30-Day Review
The Line Manager conducts a structured 30-Day Review meeting. Outcomes and action
points are recorded in Workday. The HRBP is notified of the completion.

Step F2 – 60-Day Review
A 60-Day Review is conducted by the Line Manager, with a focus on role performance
and any training needs. If performance concerns are identified, the HRBP is involved
to agree a support plan.

Step F3 – 90-Day Probation Review
The Line Manager completes the Probation Review Form in Workday and submits it to
the HRBP. Outcomes are: Probation Passed, Probation Extended (by up to 30 days),
or Probation Failed. Extension or failure requires HR Director approval. The new
employee is informed in writing via the Employee Self-Service Portal.

6. COMPLIANCE AND KEY POLICIES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

6.1 Right to Work Policy
All right-to-work checks must be completed before the employee's first day, as
required by the Immigration, Asylum and Nationality Act 2006. The Talent Acquisition
Team is responsible for retaining copies of verified documents in Workday.

6.2 GDPR – Employee Data Policy
Employee personal data collected during onboarding is processed under the Legitimate
Interests lawful basis (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f)). Data is retained for the duration
of employment plus 6 years. Employees can exercise data subject rights via the
HRBP or the Data Protection Officer (DPO).

6.3 Equal Opportunities Policy
The onboarding process must be applied consistently regardless of age, disability,
gender reassignment, race, religion, sex, or sexual orientation, in accordance with
the Equality Act 2010. Any reasonable adjustments required by the new employee must
be arranged by the Line Manager in consultation with the HRBP before Day 1.

6.4 Mandatory Training Completion Policy
All mandatory e-learning modules must be completed within the stated deadlines.
Failure to complete within 30 days of the deadline will result in system access
suspension until compliance is confirmed. The Learning Management System
automatically notifies the IT Department to trigger the suspension.

7. SYSTEMS USED
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
- Greenhouse (ATS)                   – Candidate tracking and offer management
- Workday (HRIS)                     – Employee records, onboarding workflows, probation reviews
- Sterling                           – Background screening and DBS checks
- ServiceNow (ITSM)                  – IT provisioning requests and service desk
- ADP                                – Payroll processing and payslip delivery
- Cornerstone (LMS)                  – Mandatory e-learning and compliance training
- Microsoft 365                      – Email, Teams, SharePoint collaboration
- Salesforce CRM                     – Customer data access for relevant roles
- Employee Self-Service Portal       – Self-service HR, payslips, and policy documents

8. KEY METRICS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
- Time-to-productivity: target < 90 days
- Background check completion rate before Day 1: target 100%
- Mandatory e-learning completion within deadline: target > 95%
- IT provisioning readiness by 09:00 Day 1: target 100%
- 30/60/90 Day Review completion rate: target > 90%
- New employee satisfaction score (30-day survey): target > 4.2/5.0
